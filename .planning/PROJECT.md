# Seaduced

## What This Is

A premium brand website for Seaduced — a sea moss-infused personal lubricant under the "Naturally Intimate" product line. The site will showcase the product with an elegant, oceanic aesthetic, educate visitors on sea moss benefits, and drive purchases. Built for Gina Grek (product owner) as a personal side project with Julian Bradley mentoring.

## Core Value

A visually stunning product website that makes Seaduced look premium and trustworthy enough for first-time visitors to buy — the product sells itself through the brand experience.
